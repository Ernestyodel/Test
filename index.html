What is TonFi?

Liquidity Engine

  • We provide liquidity to select TON tokens to earn trading fees and strengthen the ecosystem.
  • Fees are cycled back via buyback & burn and periodic holder rewards.

Transparent Treasury

  • Live wallet visibility so investors track holdings in real time.
  • DAO suggests and prioritizes targets via open proposals.

Community‑Driven

  • DAO voting governs major actions: new liquidity positions, buybacks, burns, and rewards cadence.
  • Focus on sustainable, long‑term value.
