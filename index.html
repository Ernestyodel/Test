How are LP fees realized?

The reserve deploys liquidity on DEXes (e.g., DeDust). Resulting LP fees accrue to the reserve and can be used to support buy‑back‑and‑burn and growth.

Can holders claim rewards without removing LP?

Yes. Holder rewards route separately from the reserve. LP positions stay staked; rewards are a distinct on‑chain distribution.

Will my LP token count increase from fees?

LP fees increase the value of the LP position over time; your LP token count is constant unless you add/remove liquidity.
