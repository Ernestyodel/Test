TonFi Whitepaper (v1.0)

Overview

TonFi is a community token on the TON blockchain focused on sustainable on-chain cashflows. A portion of trade taxes funds a treasury that deploys liquidity across TON markets to earn LP fees. Those fees help power buybacks & burns and reward long-term holders.

Mechanics

  • Buy Tax: 5% → 3% Liquidity & Buyback, 2% Holders.
  • Sell Tax: 7% → 5% Liquidity & Buyback, 2% Holders.
  • Treasury Deployments: Curated LP positions on reputable TON DEX pools; target real yield from trading fees.
  • Buyback & Burn: Periodic buybacks using accrued fees; purchased tokens are sent to burn address.
  • Holder Rewards: Automated distribution of the holder share to staking/holding participants (params disclosed at launch).

Treasury & Transparency

  • Primary treasury address: UQCa8VoKt4HcjEyhmCjY9STErQmRCqPm3n0bJYzG4dQDGLhh
  • Real-time dashboards will show the total USD value of holdings on-site, with on-chain proofs.
  • Monthly transparency notes summarizing positions, fees earned, and burns executed.

Governance

  • DAO-style proposals to suggest new tokens/pools for treasury deployments.
  • Snapshot-like off-chain signaling + on-chain execution where applicable.

Token & Launch

  • Ticker/Supply: TBA (fair, transparent, auditable).
  • Listings: Initial TON DEX pair; CEX/aggregator listings as milestones are met.

Risks

  • Market volatility can reduce LP fee yields and impact buybacks.
  • Smart-contract and integration risks mitigated by audits and conservative deployments.
  • Policy, listing, or platform changes can affect execution timelines.
