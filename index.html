What is TONFI?

TONFI is a community‑first meme token on The Open Network. A built‑in treasury (“reserve”) accumulates yield by providing liquidity to curated TON assets. Yield flows into buyback‑and‑burn to support price, and a portion is shared with holders as passive income.

  • • Transparent on‑chain treasury reporting
  • • Sustainable, utility‑aligned tokenomics
  • • Lightweight, mobile‑first site (no build tools)

FAQ

How do holder rewards work? ⌄

A portion of taxes funds LP positions. Over time, LP fees accrue and are earmarked for buybacks & burns and periodic distributions to holders. Exact cadence will be announced.

Where can I trade TONFI? ⌄

Initially on TON DEXes like DeDust or STON.fi. Links will appear on launch.

Is the treasury transparent? ⌄

Yes—anyone can view the address on Tonviewer. A public dashboard will follow.
